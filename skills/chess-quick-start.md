# Chess Game - Quick Start Guide

## How to Play

### Starting a Game

1. **Say to the AI**: "Let's play chess" or "I want to play chess"

2. **AI will ask**: "Would you like to play as White or Black?"

3. **Choose your color**: Say "White" or "Black"

4. **Board appears**: An interactive chess board shows up on the right side of the screen

5. **Start playing!**

### Making Moves (3 Ways)

#### Method 1: Drag and Drop (Easiest)
- Click and hold on your piece
- Drag it to where you want to move
- Release to complete the move
- Green indicators show valid moves

#### Method 2: Click to Move
- Click on your piece (it highlights in yellow)
- Valid moves show as green dots/rings
- Click on a green indicator to move there

#### Method 3: Voice Commands
- Say your move: "e2 to e4" or "knight to f3"
- AI executes it for you

### During the Game

**Your Turn:**
- Pieces are draggable
- Green indicators show where you can move
- Board says "Your turn!" in green

**AI's Turn:**
- Board says "AI thinking..." in blue
- AI makes its move automatically
- Then it's your turn again

### Visual Indicators

- **Yellow highlight**: Selected piece or last move
- **Green dot**: Empty square you can move to
- **Green ring**: Piece you can capture
- **Red highlight**: King in check
- **Turn indicator**: Shows whose turn it is

### Game End

- **Checkmate**: Winner announced
- **Stalemate**: Draw announced
- Board stays visible
- Ask AI to start a new game

## Tips

1. **See valid moves**: Click a piece to see where it can go
2. **Plan ahead**: Check what moves are available before moving
3. **Watch for check**: Red highlight shows when king is in danger
4. **Captured pieces**: Shown below the board
5. **Combine methods**: Drag for speed, voice for complex moves

## Example Game

```
You: "Let's play chess"
AI: "Would you like to play as White or Black?"
You: "I'll be white"
AI: "Perfect! You're White, I'm Black. You move first!"

[Interactive board appears]

You: [Drag pawn from e2 to e4]
AI: "Nice opening! I'll respond with e7 to e5..."
[AI's piece moves automatically]

You: [Drag knight from g1 to f3]
AI: "Developing pieces! I'll bring out my knight to c6..."
[AI's piece moves]

... game continues ...
```

## Troubleshooting

**Board not showing?**
- Make sure you chose a color
- Ask AI to start a chess game

**Can't drag pieces?**
- Check if it's your turn
- Make sure it's your color piece

**Move not working?**
- Piece might be pinned
- Move might be illegal
- Try clicking to see valid moves

## Have Fun!

Play naturally - drag pieces, speak moves, or click to select. The AI will play strategically and respond to your moves. All chess rules are enforced automatically. Enjoy your game! ♟️
